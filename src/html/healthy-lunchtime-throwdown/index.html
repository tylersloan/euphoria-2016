euphoria 2016 Healthy Lunchtime Throwdown

Please keep in mind the following guidelines when submitting your recipe:

1. There will be two age brackets for this year's competition. The first bracket will be for children ages 8-12 and the second will be for teenagers ages 13-16.
2. Applicants must be a student in the Greenville County School District.
3. Recipe submissions must follow USDA School Guidelines.
4. The winners recipe will be added to all Greenville County School menus. Please be aware that recipes may be subject to modification to accommodate for allergies (milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat and soybeans)
5. Please keep in mind that there are over 80,000 students in the Greenville County School District and that recipes must be budget friendly to be executed for that many students.
6. Please remember that these is a Kids Cooking Competition and that all recipes should be child developed and not parent driven.
5. Applicants must be available the day of the Final Cook-off at euphoria, September 25th.
